The Little Things Freddie Loved

Presented by Nostalgie

You probably have heard “Bohemian Rhapsody” by Queen. That song is written forty five years ago by the Rock God, Freddie Mercury. Thank’s to him, this song became a commercial success and is becoming one of the best-selling singles of all time. The world never forgets his impact to the rock history. But what else the world knows about him besides his talent and his death-tragedy?

As the tribute of his passing, we wrote about what are little things that made his life brighter, like about his interest to cats and spicy foods?
